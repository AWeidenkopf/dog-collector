About the Dog Collector

With Dog Collector, you can create a profile to your dogs and manage their feedings. A helpful tool to ensure that your pets are not being over or underfed. Start by signing up and adding all of your beloved pooches with name, breed, description and age! Then, add the feeding to the application, when you're good to go!
